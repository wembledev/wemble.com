[Fitnito](https://fitnito.com) is a simple yet powerful application that helps gym owners manage classes, clients, and passes with ease. Built with a user-friendly interface and a suite of robust features, it's designed for gym owners who want to streamline operations and grow their business, without paying enterprise prices.

### The Problem

Fitnito started with a local gym owner stuck on an expensive, complex scheduling system: roughly $500/month for features they mostly didn't use. They needed the essentials: classes, clients, passes, and recurring sessions. Nothing more.

### What We Built

Working closely with that gym owner to understand the real demands of gym management, we built a platform that kept the essential functionality while cutting the cost dramatically. Gyms using Fitnito have seen significant time savings, fewer scheduling errors, and happier clients. It now serves gyms across Canada.

### Feature Highlights

**Recurring Sessions**

Set up weekly classes or training sessions once, and the schedule populates automatically week after week. Less time on admin, fewer errors, more time with clients.

![Fitnito Dashboard - Recurring Sessions](/assets/images/fitnito-recurring-sessions.png)

**Program Assignment**

Assign specific programs to passes and sessions. Whether it's beginner yoga or advanced strength training, Fitnito lets gyms create tailored experiences for clients at every fitness level.

![Fitnito Dashboard - Passes and Programs](/assets/images/fitnito-passes-and-programs.png)

**Scheduling Built for Small Gyms**

Small gyms juggle multiple classes and individual training sessions with tiny teams. Fitnito's scheduler is intuitive for both staff and clients: no double-bookings, no scheduling errors, no training manual required.

![Fitnito Client Facing Scheduler](/assets/images/fitnito-scheduler.png)

### The Takeaway

You don't need an enterprise tool (or an enterprise bill) to run a gym well. Building directly with the person who has the problem, and cutting everything they don't need, beat the incumbent on both price and usability.
