Fitify

Live

Fitness for people who hate fitness. Daily tips that trick you into exercising by disguising workouts as regular life. Walk your dog? Cardio. Carry all the groceries in one trip? Farmer's carry. Rage-clean your kitchen? 200 calories torched.

Voice Agent

MIT

AI voice agent that answers real phone calls. Local STT/TTS on Apple Silicon with voice cloning -- clone any voice from a 10-second clip. We cloned Norm Macdonald.

DashBot

MIT

Real-time dashboard for AI agents. Chat with your agent, monitor status, and interact with agentic cards — from any browser. Built with Rails 8, React 19, and Action Cable.

Ruby · TypeScript · React · SQLite
