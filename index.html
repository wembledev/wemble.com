How one person ships like a team

Wemble isn't a typical software company. There's no team of 12. No project managers. No Jira board (thank god).

It's one developer with AI agents that handle research, write code, monitor production, and draft the write-ups. The same AI that powers our products also helps build them. We're not just using AI. We're proving what's possible when you lean all the way in.

Every product on this site was built this way. Every write-up, every open source contribution. If you're curious what that looks like in practice, read our stuff or browse the code.

Yes, it's a real corporation.

Paperwork and all. The experiment is what we do with it.
