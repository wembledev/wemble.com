Fitify

üî® IN DEVELOPMENT
fitify.ca ‚Üí

Fitness for people who hate fitness

Ever notice how fitness apps act like drill sergeants with motivational posters? "CRUSH YOUR GOALS!" "NO PAIN NO GAIN!" Thanks, but no. That stuff makes you want to delete the app and eat a pizza out of spite.

Fitify will take a different approach: disguise exercise as regular life. Walk your dog? Cardio. Carry all the groceries in one trip? Farmer's carry. Rage-clean your kitchen? 200 calories torched. The secret: you're already working out. You just don't know it yet.

Building with Rails 8 and Hotwire. Simple daily tips. Zero guilt. Sassy, not mean. The bar is hilariously low, and that's the point. Because motivation is overrated‚Äîwhat you need is permission to count that thing you just did as exercise.

DashBot

üíö OPEN SOURCE MIT
github.com/wembledev/dashbot ‚Üí

Real-time dashboard for AI agents (because terminal logs are boring)

Working with AI agents is cool. Watching them work in real-time? Even cooler. DashBot gives you a live dashboard to monitor your OpenClaw agents, see what they're thinking, watch tool calls happen, and interact with them through a clean React interface.

Built with Rails 8 (yes, Rails is still awesome), React, Action Cable for WebSockets, and OpenClaw for agent orchestration. It's the kind of tool we wanted when building our own AI stuff, so we built it and open sourced it.
